# About

IIT(BHU)CyberSec is an enthusiastic cybersecurity club and CTF team from IIT(BHU), Varanasi, India. Initially started as a CTF team in 2019 by Shreesh Roliwal and Shivansh Saini, the team has now evolved to a fully functioning club conducting CTFs, workshops and events to educate about cybersecurity. The functioning of IIT(BHU)CyberSec as a club is managed by [Club of Programmers (COPS)](https://www.copsiitbhu.co.in), where the team operates as the Infosec Vertical among other verticals.
